TradeNet offers integrated, front, middle and back office functions. TradeNet is an extreme trading application.

01

TradeNet is characterized by an extremely secure and scalable architecture that can support hundred of thousands of orders in a single trading session.

Emerging Markets have different characteristics and requirements than those of developed ones. Therefore, systems that serve brokerage firms in Emerging Markets have to be specialized to deliver a better product fit and a closer match to the needs of these markets. Additionally, companies providing these solutions should be more geared towards Emerging Markets in order to give enough priority and attention to brokerage firms in these markets.

02

As a matter of fact, 100% of our business comes from Emerging Markets. This means the company, the people, and the product are specifically geared to serve Emerging Markets.
